Nav Data Sheet

Insert grids using 2, 4, 6, 8 or 10 figure grid references. Switch between units, imperial or metric, Nato mils or degrees. Add modifiers such as terrain, time of day, and tactical situation to more accurately gauge the likely amount of time your trip will take. Add going and remarks to more vividly describe the path taken.
